# Excel Formulas 📊

## 1. SUM
Adds values  
`=SUM(A1:A10)`

---

## 2. AVERAGE. 
`=AVERAGE(A1:A10)`

---

## 3. COUNT
Counts numbers  
`=COUNT(A1:A10)`

---

## 4. IF
Conditional logic  
`=IF(A1>50,"Pass","Fail")`

---
## 5. SUMIFS
Conditional sum  
`=SUMIFS(sum_range, criteria_range, criteria)`

---

## 6. COUNTIFS
Counts with conditions  
`=COUNTIFS(range, criteria)`

---

## 7. XLOOKUP ⭐
Find value from another table  
`=XLOOKUP(lookup_value, lookup_array, return_array)`

---

## 8. VLOOKUP (old)  
`=VLOOKUP(value, table, col_index, FALSE)`

---

## 9. INDEX + MATCH
Advanced lookup  
`=INDEX(return_range, MATCH(value, lookup_range, 0))`

---

## 10. CONCAT
Combine text  
`=CONCAT(A1, B1)`
